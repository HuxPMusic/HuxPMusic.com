Biography

Mitch "Michelle" Bentz, known online as HuxP, is a genderfluid American vocal synth cover artist and producer. Active with the VOCALOID4 engine since 2017, he began releasing covers under the HuxP name in 2023.

Working mainly in J-Pop, pop and rock, he releases music through his own label HuxP Music. He is the twin brother of video creator HayBombYT. HuxP is best known for covers featuring Hatsune Miku and the UTAU voicebank Shiki Taigen, and is one of only two Shiki Taigen producers still actively releasing music.

Across three studio cover albums and several compilations, HuxP has recorded more than 90 songs. His work has reached milestones such as a Spotify "This Is" playlist, tens of thousands of streams on key tracks and a Support A Creator partnership in Fortnite using the code HUXP. In mid 2025, his catalog was removed from major streaming services for editorial reasons outside of his control, but new projects continue on.
