About HuxP

HuxP is an American cover producer who primarily uses VOCALOID and UTAU voicebanks. He has used the VOCALOID4 engine since 2017 and started releasing covers in 2023. HuxP is also a member of the side projects DOCHIMERA and LockedStep , contributing vocals, arrangements, and collaborative covers in both collectives.
