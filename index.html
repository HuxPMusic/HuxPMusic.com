Biography

Mitch "Michelle" Bentz, known online as HuxP, is an American vocal synth cover artist, producer, webmaster, and digital creative. He has been active with vocal synth software since 2017 and began publicly releasing covers under the HuxP name in 2023.

His music work centers on VOCALOID and UTAU, especially projects featuring Hatsune Miku and Shiki Taigen. Across his covers, videos, and independent releases, HuxP focuses on J-POP, pop, rock, and internet-era music culture, with an emphasis on presentation, archiving, and clean metadata.

Outside of music, he also builds fan and personal web projects, maintains online archives, and works across art, design, and site development. HuxP Music serves as the home for current releases, videos, and related projects.
