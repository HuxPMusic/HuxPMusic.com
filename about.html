About HuxP

Profile

Mitch “Michelle” Bentz (he/she), born March 22, 2008. Vocal synth cover producer since 2017, publishing covers publicly since 2023.

Music & Style

Known for producing covers with Hatsune Miku and Shiki Taigen. Albums include Vocarips, Vocadiva, and Miku + Michelle. Among the few actively using the Shiki Taigen voicebank.

Highlights

Cover of “初音ミクの消失” reached 50,000 Spotify plays. In April 2025, became a Fortnite-affiliated creator with Support‑A‑Creator code “HUXP”.

Collaborations

Frequently collaborates with twin brother HayBombYT. Co-creator of the band LockedStep.
