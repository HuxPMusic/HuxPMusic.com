About

I'm Mitch "Michelle" Bentz, and I create under the name HuxP. I've worked with vocal synth software since 2017 and started releasing covers publicly as HuxP in 2023.

My music work focuses on VOCALOID and UTAU, especially projects featuring Hatsune Miku and Shiki Taigen. I mostly cover J-pop, pop, and rock, and I care a lot about clean presentation and metadata.

Outside of music, I maintain archives, build fan and personal websites, and do visual/site design work.

This site is still evolving. New sections, layout changes, and project pages may be added over time as the site grows.
