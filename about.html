About HuxP

HuxP, also known as Mitch or Michelle, is an American VOCALOID and UTAU cover producer who has been active since 2017. They began publicly releasing covers in 2023 under the name HuxP (previously hux39). Their work is characterized by strong attention to detail, audio remastering, and personal reimaginings of popular and niche songs alike.

Since releasing their first album Vocarips in 2023, HuxP has published multiple projects across Bandcamp, with each release accompanied by cover artwork and character theming.

Outside of music production, HuxP maintains an organized discography via VocaDB, contributes metadata to MusicBrainz, and shares content through their personal websites. Their work often bridges fan culture, archival interest, and stylistic homage to early 2000s internet media.
